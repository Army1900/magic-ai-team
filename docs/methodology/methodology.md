# OpenTeam Methodology

## Objective
- Translate user intent into an executable AI team with clear goals, roles, and guardrails.

## Core Stages
1. Discover intent and constraints.
2. Design team topology (agents, skills, MCPs, model policy).
3. Validate policy and compatibility.
4. Simulate and evaluate.
5. Optimize and export.

## Guardrails
- Never bypass policy failures by default.
- Keep generated plans auditable and reversible.
- Prefer simple topologies before scaling complexity.
